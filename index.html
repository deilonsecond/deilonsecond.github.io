About Us

PIGANG TAYABAS AGRI SERVICES INC. is a food Manufacturing and Exporting firm focused on providing customized solutions for an ever changing food and food supplement industry. The result of a harmony of talents and experiences shared by its founders who boast over three decades of combined experience in manufacturing, research and development, logistics, engineering, plant operations and finance.

Not only do we offer customizable tolling solution, we also produce natural ingredients based on the freshest produce which we source direct from farm gate partners.

Our Story

Our story began with a passion for farming and a love for all things fresh and natural. We started our company with a small farm, growing a variety of fruits and vegetables. As our farm grew, so did our love for creating delicious and healthy products from the fruits we grew.

One day, we had the idea to start extracting juice from our fruits and bottling it to share with others. From that moment on, we never looked back. We invested in the best equipment and began experimenting with different fruit combinations to create unique and flavorful juices.

Our juices are made from 100% fresh fruits, with no added sugars or preservatives. We believe that good health starts with what we eat and drink, and we are proud to offer a product that is not only delicious but also good for you.

As our company continues to grow, we remain committed to using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients. We are grateful for the support we have received from our customers and look forward to sharing our love of fresh juice with many more people in the future.

Our Company Mission is to be the first and foremost fresh puree and ingredient supplier in the country. Providing high quality purees and fruits sourced directly from our farmer partners. It is our desire to foster good long lasting business relationships with our clients by giving exemplary services that are on time, of the right quality and of the right price.

Services

Our Purees are processed from fruits directly after harvest. We achieve this by positioning our processing plants near farm-gate to ensure freshness and consistency of supply while dealing directly with farmers to eliminate the middleman and deliver good quality at competitive rates.

Our Featured Fruits / Vegetables

Pomelo

Pomelo is a rich source of vitamin C, antioxidants, and dietary fibers. Vitamin C is important for maintaining a healthy immune system, while antioxidants can help protect the body against damage from free radicals. The dietary fibers in pomelo can aid in digestion and promote feelings of fullness.

Calamansi

Kalamansi is a good source of vitamin C and antioxidants. Vitamin C is important for maintaining a healthy immune system and promoting healthy skin. The antioxidants found in Kalamansi can also help protect the body from damage from free radicals.

Pineapple

Pineapple is a good source of vitamin C, manganese, and bromelain. Vitamin C is important for maintaining a healthy immune system, and manganese is important for bone health. Bromelain is an enzyme that can aid in digestion, and may also have anti-inflammatory properties.

Guyabano

Guyabano is a good source of vitamin C, antioxidants and dietary fibers. Vitamin C is important for maintaining a healthy immune system, while antioxidants can help protect the body against damage from free radicals. The dietary fibers found in guyabano can aid in digestion and promote feelings of fullness. It is also believed to have anti-cancer properties, however this is not yet confirmed by scientific studies.
